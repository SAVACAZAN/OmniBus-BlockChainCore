Omni\+Bus DEX supports two parallel trading modes:\+ {\bfseries{real}} (production money) and {\bfseries{paper}} (demo/\+simulation). Both use the same matching engine, orderbook format, and REST endpoint shapes. The only differences are the URL prefix, the settlement asset, and trade-\/log isolation.\hypertarget{md_wiki-kimi-omnibus_202-_a_p_i-_r_e_f_e_r_e_n_c_e_autotoc_md11146}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{Real vs Paper comparison}{Real vs Paper comparison}}\label{md_wiki-kimi-omnibus_202-_a_p_i-_r_e_f_e_r_e_n_c_e_autotoc_md11146}

\begin{DoxyTable}{3}{}{}{1}

Route prefix  &{\ttfamily /\+exchange/\+0/\+\texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}}  &{\ttfamily /\+paper/\+0/\+\texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}}  \\
Settlement asset  &{\ttfamily OMNI}  &{\ttfamily OMNI\+\_\+\+DEMO}  \\

Switching from paper to real requires changing exactly one URL prefix.\hypertarget{md_wiki-kimi-omnibus_202-_a_p_i-_r_e_f_e_r_e_n_c_e_autotoc_md11149}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{Paper balance tracking}{Paper balance tracking}}\label{md_wiki-kimi-omnibus_202-_a_p_i-_r_e_f_e_r_e_n_c_e_autotoc_md11149}
Paper balances are tracked inside the {\ttfamily exchange\+\_\+paper} engine\textquotesingle{}s internal ledger, not on the blockchain. The {\ttfamily owner} parameter (trader address) is still required for private endpoints because the engine uses the same address format as real trading. Demo balances can be seeded via:\+


\begin{DoxyItemize}

\item Agent-\/native bootstrap faucet ({\ttfamily claim\+\_\+faucet} with paper mode)
\end{DoxyItemize}\hypertarget{md_wiki-kimi-omnibus_202-_a_p_i-_r_e_f_e_r_e_n_c_e_autotoc_md11150}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{Withdraw behaviour in paper mode}{Withdraw behaviour in paper mode}}\label{md_wiki-kimi-omnibus_202-_a_p_i-_r_e_f_e_r_e_n_c_e_autotoc_md11150}
{\ttfamily Withdraw} in paper mode does not create an on-\/chain transaction. It deducts the amount from the paper internal ledger and returns a synthetic {\ttfamily refid}. The funds never leave the demo system.\hypertarget{md_wiki-kimi-omnibus_202-_a_p_i-_r_e_f_e_r_e_n_c_e_autotoc_md11151}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{Escrow address in demo mode}{Escrow address in demo mode}}\label{md_wiki-kimi-omnibus_202-_a_p_i-_r_e_f_e_r_e_n_c_e_autotoc_md11151}
{\ttfamily exchange\+\_\+get\+Escrow\+Address} returns the same escrow address for both modes (the bridge/\+registrar slot). In paper mode, deposits to this address are not processed on-\/chain; they are credited to the paper internal ledger by the demo grant mechanism.
