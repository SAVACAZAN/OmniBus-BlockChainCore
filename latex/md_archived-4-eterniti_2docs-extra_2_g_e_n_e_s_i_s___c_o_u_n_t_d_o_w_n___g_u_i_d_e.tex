Genesis Countdown is the {\bfseries{initialization sequence}} for Omni\+Bus blockchain. It displays:\+
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Real-\/time blockchain status
\item Connected miners
\item When genesis mining will start
\item Live miner statistics
\item Ability to launch multiple light miners simultaneously
\end{DoxyItemize}

{\bfseries{Target:\+}} Launch with minimum 3 light miners connected, then begin mining genesis blocks.
