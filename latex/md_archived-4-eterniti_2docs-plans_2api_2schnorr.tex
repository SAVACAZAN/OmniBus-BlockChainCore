BIP-\/340 Schnorr Signatures over secp256k1
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item 64-\/byte signatures (vs 71 for ECDSA DER)
\item Linear:\+ sig(m, sk1) + sig(m, sk2) = sig(m, sk1+sk2) → enables Mu\+Sig2
\item Batch verification:\+ verify N sigs faster than N individual verifications
\item Provably secure under DL assumption in Random Oracle Model
\end{DoxyItemize}
