A fast and minimal alternative to globby and fast-\/glob, meant to behave the same way.

Both globby and fast-\/glob present some behavior no other globbing lib has, which makes it hard to manually replace with something smaller and better.

This library uses only two subdependencies, compared to {\ttfamily globby}\textquotesingle{}s \href{https://npmgraph.js.org/?q=globby@16.2.0}{\texttt{23}} and {\ttfamily fast-\/glob}\textquotesingle{}s \href{https://npmgraph.js.org/?q=fast-glob@3.3.3}{\texttt{17}}.\hypertarget{dir_db887bd1abd4b369b9bea0c1f7a2a1f7_autotoc_md10665}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{Usage}{Usage}}\label{dir_db887bd1abd4b369b9bea0c1f7a2a1f7_autotoc_md10665}
