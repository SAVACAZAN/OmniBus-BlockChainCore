\hypertarget{dir_dfd45931960216c0879534fc4a501f88_autotoc_md9230}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{tinyglobby}{tinyglobby}}\label{dir_dfd45931960216c0879534fc4a501f88_autotoc_md9230}
\href{https://npmjs.com/package/tinyglobby}{\texttt{}} \href{https://npmjs.com/package/tinyglobby}{\texttt{}}

A fast and minimal alternative to globby and fast-\/glob, meant to behave the same way.

Both globby and fast-\/glob present some behavior no other globbing lib has, which makes it hard to manually replace with something smaller and better.

This library uses only two subdependencies, compared to {\ttfamily globby}\textquotesingle{}s \href{https://npmgraph.js.org/?q=globby@14.1.0}{\texttt{23}} and {\ttfamily fast-\/glob}\textquotesingle{}s \href{https://npmgraph.js.org/?q=fast-glob@3.3.3}{\texttt{17}}.\hypertarget{dir_dfd45931960216c0879534fc4a501f88_autotoc_md9231}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{Usage}{Usage}}\label{dir_dfd45931960216c0879534fc4a501f88_autotoc_md9231}
