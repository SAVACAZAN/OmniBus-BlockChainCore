New Findings

SEV-75 | HIGH | rpc_server.zig:~16224 | Protocol/Validation Bypass
Blockchain.mempool is array_list.Managed(Transaction) in blockchain.zig:238.
All RPC handlers call ctx.bc.mempool.append(tx) -- raw ArrayList.append().
Bypassed: sig verify, fee min (1 SAT), TX size (100 KB), total (1 MB), dup hash, RBF.
Exploit: Send gov_vote RPC with fee=0 and zeroed sig. TX mined without sig check.
Fix: Unify Blockchain.mempool to use Mempool struct, or replace all appends with Mempool.add().

SEV-76 | MEDIUM | p2p.zig:~2374 | DoS
P2PNode.checkRateLimit() defined but never called in dispatch loop.
Exploit: Peer sends 10k+ msgs/sec. CPU saturated, mining halts, ban never triggered.
Fix: Add checkRateLimit() call before dispatchMessage().

SEV-77 | MEDIUM | mempool.zig:87 | DoS
Mempool.add() has no per-sender TX cap. One addr fills 1 MB mempool (0.000005 OMNI cost).
Fix: MAX_TX_PER_SENDER = 25 enforced in Mempool.add() after dup detection.
